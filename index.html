How Does Leaseum Work?

Leaseum Partners will issue Leaseum (LSM) tokens and invest the proceeds through a traditional Fund focused on Core-Plus commercial real estate located in US tier 2 cities.


Such digital proof of ownership of the Fund's interest can be traded at low cost 24/7, which allows investors to bypass standard 5+year lock-up periods imposed within the commercial real estate industry.


Real assets-backed Leaseum Tokens generate regular income with defensive qualities.
